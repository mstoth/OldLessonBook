Using the Lesson Detail Page

The Lesson Detail Page has several functions.

The four icons on the top do the following:

  Change the date of the lesson.

  Print to an AirPrint Printer

  Email the lesson to Student.

Send the lesson to student over
a Bluetooth Link.

You can delete the lesson by selecting the
trash can in the upper right hand corner.

When you start a new lesson, usually you
will want to copy the previous lesson.
The lowest button does this.

Adding and removing pieces is done with the
buttons labeled as such.
