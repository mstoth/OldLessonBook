Adding a Piece

As you add pieces to a lesson, your database
of pieces grows.  You should only have to
enter a piece once by typing the values in. 
After that you can choose it from the picker. 

The left sideof the picker is the title of the
piece and the right side is the composer.
If you change the  composer, only that
composer's pieces will be displayed on the
left side of the picker.

Selecting the difficulty bar values will limit
the choices to pieces with those levels of
difficulty.  It does not have to be used.  It is
only there as a convenience if desired.

Pressing "Clear" clears all the fields and the
difficulty bar.
